# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

**Orvexa by KAGEYOSHI** is committed to fostering an open, welcoming, and inclusive environment for the **OYI - Open in your IDE** project. We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of:

- Age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression
- Level of experience, education, socio-economic status
- Nationality, personal appearance, race, religion
- Sexual identity and orientation

## Our Standards

### ✅ Positive Behavior

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment:

- **Respect**: Using welcoming and inclusive language
- **Empathy**: Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- **Constructive Feedback**: Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- **Community Focus**: Focusing on what is best for the community
- **Collaboration**: Showing empathy towards other community members
- **Professional Conduct**: Maintaining professional and courteous communication

### ❌ Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- **Harassment**: The use of sexualized language or imagery, unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- **Trolling**: Insulting/derogatory comments, personal or political attacks
- **Discrimination**: Public or private harassment based on protected characteristics
- **Privacy Violations**: Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- **Inappropriate Content**: Any other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
- **Spam**: Excessive self-promotion or off-topic posts

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces related to the **OYI - Open in your IDE** project, including:

- GitHub repository (issues, pull requests)
- Project documentation and wikis
- Social media channels and communications
- Email communications with project maintainers
- Community events and meetups (virtual or in-person)

This Code of Conduct also applies when an individual is representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by contacting:

- **Primary**: orvexa.conduct@kageyoshi.com
- **GitHub**: Use the "Report abuse" feature
- **Alternative**: Create a private issue in the repository

All reports will be handled with discretion and confidentiality.

### Investigation Process

1. **Acknowledgment**: Reports will be acknowledged within 24 hours
2. **Investigation**: We will review the incident and gather relevant information
3. **Decision**: Appropriate action will be determined based on the violation
4. **Communication**: All parties will be informed of the outcome
5. **Appeal**: A process for appeals will be provided if needed

### Enforcement Actions

Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to:

- Remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, and issues
- Ban temporarily or permanently any contributor for behaviors deemed inappropriate
- Take other actions they deem appropriate for the health of the community

#### Graduated Response

1. **Warning**: Private warning about inappropriate behavior
2. **Temporary Suspension**: Temporary ban from project participation
3. **Permanent Ban**: Permanent removal from the community

### Appeals Process

If you believe an enforcement action was taken in error:

1. **Email**: orvexa.conduct@kageyoshi.com within 30 days
2. **Include**: Details of the incident and why you believe the action was incorrect
3. **Review**: The appeal will be reviewed by a different maintainer
4. **Decision**: Final decision will be communicated within 14 days

## Responsibilities

### For Community Members

- **Follow** the Code of Conduct in all project spaces
- **Report** violations when you see them
- **Support** a welcoming environment for all participants
- **Respect** decisions made by project maintainers

### For Project Maintainers

- **Model** positive behavior and lead by example
- **Enforce** the Code of Conduct fairly and consistently
- **Address** reports promptly and thoroughly
- **Protect** the privacy of those who report violations
- **Take** appropriate action to maintain community standards
